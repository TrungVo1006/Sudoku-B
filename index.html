In this experiment, you will solve mini Sudoku puzzles.

The rules of Sudoku are to fill in all the digits so that no duplicates appear in any row, column or bold-bordered square.

This version of the interface uses mouse and keyboard To write a number, click on a handwritten number on the side, or press number button on your keyboard(1-9) then click on a square. The program will return to eraser atomatically.

Press d for next puzzle, a to go back to previous puzzle, enter to check, c to clear the board. Hold ctrl for small number
